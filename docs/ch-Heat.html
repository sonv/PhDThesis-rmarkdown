MATHEMATICAL INVESTIGATIONS OF SOME PHYSICAL MODELS

2 Optimal heat transfer

2.1 Motivations

Heat transfer is important in both daily life and the industry. Almost everyone in the United States has either an air-conditioner or a heater; nuclear plants and data centers need sophisticated heat transfer systems to stay cool.
